High Octane

What is High Octane?

High Octane is a game created by a car enthusiast with the goal to create a fun mini game to test car lovers knowledge of fun statistics of many supercars. ENJOY!

How to play:


Well it's simple really, choose a game the same way you opened this 'how to play section' by clicking on 1 of the 3 games of your choice.


Once the game is open the game will display 2 random cars it will display one cars power/speed/price and hide the other cars.


Next decide if you think the car with the hidden information will be higher or lower than the car that you can see and press on the higher or lower button in the center of the screen.
