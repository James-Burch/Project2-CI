High Octane

What is High Octane?

High Octane is a simple game for car lovers to test their knowledge of different car make and models power and performance figures.

How to play:

To play the higher or lower games simply look at the car on the right and decide if you believe it to have more or less power or 0-60mph time than the car on the left, if you think higher select the higher button or lower select the lower button, if you are correct the game will continue and you will then have to guess if the next car is higher or lower than the last. For the game guessing the bhp simple enter a whole number between 1 and 1000 and click submit (or enter on a computer). If you are within 10bhp then you will get the answer correct, if you are further than 10bhp from the answer it will prompt you to guess higher or lower.
